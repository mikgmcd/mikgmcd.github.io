About

Smilley is the DIY-musical-passion-project of songwriter/musician/person/obsessive music freak Miles Platt, which he has been writing for since 2019. His body of work consists of emotional, raw, and nostalgic alternative rock, and he strives to provide people with a space to be themselves through his self-expression.

Smilley's origins can be traced back to Miles' experiences in theatre from a young age. While acting always entertained him, he was always drawn to theatricality and performance. His background in theatre mixed with his ear for a great melody and attention to detail in his instrumentals give Smilley a distinct sound - one that passes as alternative rock but with added tinges of emotional depth, vulnerability, and an identity that is linked to Platt himself. Sometimes silly, sometimes loud, and always honest, Smilley is the perfect storm concocted of catchiness and unwavering emotional honesty. Platt also prides himself in Smilley being a celebration of all things DIY. His debut album, Stay Well!, was written, recorded, mixed, and mastered in various bedrooms and houses. Not only is Platt's music charming and silly at times, but he strives to make Smilley an open invitation for all to feel comfortable and seen. Whether through lyrics, instrumentals, or at shows, Miles values inclusivity and acceptance above all else and feels as though he "did his job successfully" if even one person steps away from his music feeling like they were understood or represented on any level.

At the end of the day, Smilley is a facade for Miles to come to terms with his own emotions. While the music is unmistakably linked to him, the earwormy hooks mixed with thought-provoking instrumentals and powerful lyrics of Smilley will make for a listening experience unlike any other.
